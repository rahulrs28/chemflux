CHEMFLUX 6.0

The Department of Chemical Engineering presents to you the 5th edition of the national level technical symposium in association with Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. Having impacted over 1500+ students in its previous editions, we have created a benchmark for Chemical Engineering Fests throughout the country.Every edition of ours has a particular ideology perfectly embodied to it aiming at fostering the importance of technical knowledge, encouraging entrepreneurship by giving opportunities to young technocrats. This year with the theme of Advancements in Chemical Technology and futuristic applications, we try to look at the new trends emerging in this field and our future prospects. Join us in witnessing a whole new level of intriguing Competitions, Panel Discussions, and for many other fun-filled events.
