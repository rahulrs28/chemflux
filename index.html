About ChemFlux 2018

The Department of Chemical Engineering presents to you the 5th edition of the national level technical symposium in association with Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. Having impacted over 1500+ students in its previous editions, we have created a benchmark for Chemical Engineering Fests throughout the country.Every edition of ours has a particular ideology perfectly embodied to it aiming at fostering the importance of technical knowledge, encouraging entrepreneurship by giving opportunities to young technocrats. This year with the theme of Advancements in Chemical Technology and futuristic applications, we try to look at the new trends emerging in this field and our future prospects. Join us for witnessing the whole new level of intriguing Competitions, Panel Discussions, and for many other fun-filled events.

CHEMAZE

Technical quiz event.

Chemaze is a technical quiz event where the teams are challenged not only based on their present knowledge, but also on their cognitive skills. The participants should come in a team of maximum 3 members. The topics can be present day developments in technology, technical subjects, scientists and scientific innovations.

  • Preliminary Rounds:
    • Choose your fate:
      Pick your interesting subject from the stick board and you’ll given a set of questions out of it.
    • Chemplay:
      It’s a video based Round were the participants will be posed with questions at the end of the video.
    • Chemstation:
      A station which will be awarding only those who segregate a set of 16 hard chemical engineering terminologies into a set of 4 each containing 4 words each.

  • Final Rounds:

    • Connection:
      Connect your wisdom with the pictures and find the terminologies
    • Arise&Awake:
      A rapid fire round

RECALIBRATE

  • First round:

    Case Study:

    A team of 4-5 members will be made to choose a card which will present them with a problem. This can be the analysis of a project, campaign or company were one has to identify a situation recommend solutions to those factors which caused the failure or success. The team which comes with the best idea will pass through the first round.

    • Second Round:

    Panel discussion:

    The individuals from the selected team will be given job designation based on the situation. In this round, they will be required to substantiate their idea. The team which comes with the most feasible but yet effective solution will emerge as the winner!

Paper & Poster Presentation


    1. Instructions
    2. Topics
    3. Suggested Layout
We are at a stage as a country where we have been blessed with resources, but we don't have the knowledge to put them in good use. And who are the people brimming with ideas? The students' fraternity. So we have come to give their ideas and innovation a platform which would help them to give wings to their ideas and fly high with bright colours. With our 10 varied topics where a student can do research in ways to change the face of technology such as fuel optimisation, advancements in reaction chemistry, waste to energy conservation, and so on, they would face a healthy competition to prove the worth of their ideas and application in real-time.

For poster presentation :
  • Poster size: The poster has to be printed in A3 or A2 size.
  • Word limit: The abstract to be sent should have 100 to 150 words.
  • Font: The font to be used in abstract needs to be Times New Roman
  • Font size: 12 and the title font size should be 14.

For paper presentation :
  • Team size: Participants are allowed to make teams consisting of upto 3 members.
  • Word limit: The abstract should not exceed 100 words.
  • Style: The abstract should follow font as Times New Roman, and font size as 12. The content of the paper should be justified in accordance to the topic selected by the team.

Topics could be selected from any one of the following:

  • 1. Fuel Optimisation.
  • 2. Advancements in Reaction Chemistry.
  • 3. Waste to Energy Conservation.
  • 4. Separation Techniques.
  • 5. Green Chemistry.
  • 6. Advanced Water Treatment Methodology.
  • 7. Fluid Mechanics and Industrial Applications.
  • 8. Heat Transfer through Nanoscale.
  • 9. Bioprocess Engineering.
The abstracts should to be sent via email before 7th March, 2018.
